Personal website.

I am currently a PhD student at the University of Angers under the supervision of Kilian Raschel (CNRS, Laboratoire Angevin de RÉcherche en MAthématiques) and Pierre Tarrago (Sorbonne University, Laboratoire de Probabilités, Statistiques et Modélisation).
My research interests lie in the Theory of Stochastic processes, more concretely the study of random walks. For my doctoral thesis I am working on random walks conditioned to stay in a cone.
